## Short CV

- **2025–present** — PhD student in Theoretical Physics at IFIMAC *(FPI-IFIMAC – funded)*
- **2024–2025** — M.Sc. in Condensed Matter Physics (UAM, Madrid). Master’s thesis on high-Chern-number magnetic topological insulators coupled to superconductivity. ([Repository](https://github.com/AlejandroSGomez/TFM_TSC-in-HighChernNumber-systems))
- **2019–2024** — Double B.Sc. in Physics & Mathematics (UCM, Madrid)
- **2023–2024** — Research internship (IEM/CSIC, Madrid): driven (Floquet) phenomena in monolayer transition-metal dichalcogenides under short laser pulses. ([Paper](https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/2515-7639/ade292/pdf))
- **Summer 2023** — Research internship (DIPC, Donostia): semi-Dirac electronic structures in 3D materials and their response to external fields
